​parsnips tart​

you will need: ​
  • 4 parsnips
  • 3oz of ham
  • 10oz of mince
  • 470g of chocolate
  • 190g sweet pastry
Instructions ​
  1. Pre-heat oven to 220°C
  2. Shred the parsnips
  3. Deseed the ham
  4. Grate the mince
  5. Eat the chocolate
  6. Grease the pie dish
  7. Slice the ham
  8. Slice the parsnips
  9. Slice the mince
  10. Cover the parsnips
  11. Recycle the ham
  12. Line the dish with the pastry
  13. Add the filling
  14. Bake for 55 minutes
